The map up above shows a map of the geographic locations pulled for people on Twitter using the terms "Austin" and "music" together in the same tweet. We can see many locations pop-up in the Central Texas region, of course, and surprisingly also quite a few going up into the northeast of the country. However, we don't tend to see a large amount of references outside of the country. It would probably make more sense for a place that is to be considered the "captial" of all live music around the globe to be referenced when discussing music a little more on one of the largest social media platforms there is.
